Electronic Navigation
====================================

Electronic Navigation refers to electronic equipment that forms an integral part of the planning, navigation and monitoring process. While they began as a supplementary means of navigation, they are quickly supplanting many traditional elements of navigation. Nonetheless, it's important to understand the mechanical and mathematical navigation that underpins these technologies.
